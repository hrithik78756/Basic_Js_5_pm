1. Number Sign Checker

Write a program that checks whether a number is positive, negative, or zero.

2. Even or Odd

Take a number as input and check whether it is even or odd.

3. Voting Eligibility

Ask the user for their age and print whether they are eligible to vote (18 or above) or not.

4. Largest of Two Numbers

Compare two numbers and print the greater number.

5. Grade Calculator

Take marks as input and print grades: .90-100 → A .75-89 → B .50-74 → C .Below 50 → Fail

6. Leap Year checker

Check whether a given year is a leap year or not.

7. Simple Calculator (switch case)

Take two numbers and an operator (+, -, *, /). Use switch-case to perform the calculation.

8. Character Type Checker

Take a character input and check if it is: .A vowel, .A consonant, or .Not a letter.

9. Discount on Shopping

If shopping amount is: .More than 500 → 20% discount .200-500 → 10% discount .Below 200 → No discount Print final price after discount.

10. Traffic Light Simulation

Take input as "red", "yellow", or "green". Display appropriate message: .Red → Stop .Yellow → Get Ready .Green → Go
